NOURISH GROUP INDONESIA - ORGANIZATIONAL TAXONOMY
This document contains the official structural mapping of scopes, departments, positions, and ranks for PT Nourish Group Indonesia. This taxonomy drives the Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) matrix and workflow approval authorities within NourishOS.

---

🏢 1. Organizational Scopes & Outlets
The organization is divided into two operational scopes:
Headquarters (HQ) / Group Level: Centralized governance, strategic division heads, and cross-outlet senior management.
Outlets / Retail Level: On-the-ground execution units including standard Restaurant concepts and Wholefoods specialty spaces.
